What is Linux?

Linux is a powerful, open-source operating system known for its speed, security, and flexibility. It’s perfect for both beginners and experts alike, offering unparalleled customization and control.

Why Use Linux?

Experience unmatched speed, security, and reliability. Linux is efficient, free, and backed by a strong community. Whether you’re learning or coding, Linux empowers you to achieve more.
